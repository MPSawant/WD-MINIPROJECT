Medicine Information

EXIZOL 2% SHAMPOO



Exizol 2% Shampoo helps in the treatment of fungal and yeast infections of the body. It belongs to the drug group known as azole antifungals. The medication prevents the growth of various types of fungi by disrupting the production of the membranes that surrounds the fungal cells. It is available in the form of a tablet that can be taken orally.

What EXIZOL ANTIDANDRUFF is used for?

  • It is used to treat and prevent dandruff and dry or greasy scaling of the scalp (seborrhoeic dermatitis)
  • It is also used to treat the inflammation associated with dandruff, relieving scalp redness and itching

Talk to your doctor before using this medicine, if you:

  • are allergic to ketoconazole or to any other contents of this medicine
  • are pregnant or breast-feeding

Note: This product is for use in adults and adolescents over 12 years of age

  • Always use this medicine exactly as your doctor has told you
  • For external use only
  • Use every 3 or 4 days for 2 - 4 weeks
  • If your scalp has not cleared within 4 weeks, speak to your doctor
  • If the shampoo gets into your eyes wash with plenty of water

Like all medicines, this medicine can cause side effects, although not everybody gets them.

SERIOUS SIDE EFFECTS

  • severe allergic reactions, including swelling of the face, lips, tongue or throat

UNCOMMON

  • application site reactions including itching, irritation, blistering, red, dry or tender skin or a skin burning sensation
  • inflamed hair follicles, hair loss or changes in hair texture
  • increased tear production
  • increased tear production

RARE

  • Eye irritation
  • Acne
  • Skin inflammation (dermatitis)
  • Flaking skin
